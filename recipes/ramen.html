Ramen

Description

Ramen is a beloved Japanese noodle soup renowned for its rich broth, chewy noodles, and diverse toppings. It combines umami flavors with a variety of ingredients, making it a comforting and customizable dish enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Broth: pork, chicken, seafood, miso, or soy sauce
  • Noodles
  • Toppings: protein (boiled, marinated eggs or slices of chasu pork) and vegetables (bamboo shoots, spinach, seaweed)
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Prepare Broth: Simmer pork bones or chicken with aromatics and umami boosters until flavorful.
  2. Cook Noodles: Boil ramen noodles until tender, then drain and rinse.
  3. Assemble Ramen: Reheat broth, season with soy sauce or miso. Divide noodles into bowls, add hot broth, top with chashu pork, eggs, and vegetables.
  4. Serve: Garnish with nori and scallions, and enjoy hot.
